# Finance-blog
Welcome to our financial analysis blog created by analysts of FIN205 – Data Technologies for Financial Modelling. Our team consists of students who collaborate to research companies, analyse financial data, and present insights
For this blog, we've decided to focus on two prominent technological companies: Nvidia and Apple. Both companies play key roles in the global technological economy, although they focus on different areas of innovation. Nvidia is well-known for its leadership in graphics processing units (GPUs) and artificial intelligence computing, whereas Apple is known for its extensive ecosystem of consumer devices and digital services.
By focusing on these two organisations, we hope to learn how differing business models, growth plans, and market conditions affect financial performance.
Disclaimer: Everything in this blog is strictly for learning purposes only and should not be taken as financial advice.
